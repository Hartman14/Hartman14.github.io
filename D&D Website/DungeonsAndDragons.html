About Us

This is a website that I've developed due to my friends and I having rather tricky schedules to plan D&D around. Hence I've made this site to help users manage their characters and set a true meta of sorts. All classes, races, items and spells on this site have been run by a tribunal consisting of myself and a panel of of other D&D players to balance everything with in a specified Meta. To submit a class, race, item or spell to the tribunal it must shown on either D&D wiki, a player handbook or another valid D&D source (everything not from a phb will be subject to balancing).
