There exists a void of death and despair, rumored to harbor horrors beyond imagination... It only begs the question, what would happen if Science and Magic were to conceive new life in such a place? More over, what would happen to those beyond void...

Kra'Kel is a voidling born of such experiments for the sole purpose of destroying the world that wronged his creator. Formed from dark magics and genetic manipulation of the extremely rare Baron Nashor cells, a new horror more destructive and powerful than those to come before it was made.

Upon Kra'Kel's release into runeterra he ravaged much of ancient Noxus, resulting in him being magically bound and sealed within a heavily guarded tome. However, due to his body's acidic nature the chains that bound him did not stand the test of time. Free from his bindings Kra'Kel slew his captors and set out to find the one whom inprisioned him. As he searches, he leaves nothing but a baron was land in his wake; the one who imprisioned him can only hope to be luck enough to suffer the same fate.
